About KICL

KICL is new, fresh, rejuvenated and customer-centric business owned by Nigerians, for essentially Nigerians. Our focus is to take ordinary activities in business and create extraordinary products and services that will not just excite our numerous consumers but will keep them coming back. We have assembled a great team to deliver on these products and services and the comsumers are attesting to our “KICL way” of delivering on our goals. We understand there are boundaries and regulations and our corporate strategy derives from ensuring utmost compliance with government and societal expectations
